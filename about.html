ABOUT US

OUR STORY

How to turn regular companies
into business success stories

When we started in the IT industry in 2014, technical recruitment looked very different from today's complex landscape. Our founding team of five specialists noticed something important: even companies with big goals and resources often struggled simply because they couldn't find the right talent and properly manage it.

Over a decade, our small office serving local clients has grown into a global operation involving over 40 IT and HR professionals. We expanded beyond recruitment into outsourcing, data analysis, people management, and tech-driven HR solutions by listening to what our clients actually needed.

The global landscape has changed a lot since we started, but our core belief remains unchanged: people are the bridge between aspirations and success. A company is not a unit, but a human collective, and the right people management is what makes any company move in the only right direction: forward.
